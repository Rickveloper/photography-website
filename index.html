Based in Portland, Maine, Rick Fryou has spent over a decade documenting the ever-changing moods of the Atlantic coastline. His work focuses on the intersection of natural forces and human presence in these marginal spaces.

Using primarily Canon equipment, Rick's photographic approach combines technical precision with an intuitive response to weather and light conditions. His images have been featured in regional publications and exhibited in galleries throughout New England.

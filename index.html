About Rich

Landscape photography from coastal Maine—fog, nor'easters, and four brutal, beautiful seasons.

Based in York, Maine, Rich has spent over a decade documenting the ever-changing moods of the Atlantic coastline. His work focuses on the intersection of natural forces and human presence in these marginal spaces.

Using primarily Sony a6300, Rich's photographic approach combines technical precision with an intuitive response to weather and light conditions.
